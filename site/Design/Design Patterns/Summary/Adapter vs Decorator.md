## Intent

* Adapter
    * Interface Conversion
* Decorator
    * Functional enhancement or weakening

## Target

* Adapter
    * between irrelevant classes
* Decorator
    * between sibling classes

## Situation

* Adapter
    * Whenever
* Decorator
    * Maintain old modules

## Extensible

* Adapter
    * Easy to add or remove
* Decorator
    * Always be careful
